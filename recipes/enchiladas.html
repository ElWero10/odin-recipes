Chicken Enchiladas

Ingredients

  • Corn Tortilla
  • Queso Fresco
  • Chicken
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Onion
  • Lettuce

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Chop Onions and Lettuce
  3. Dip tortilla in oil then into tomato sauce
  4. Let tortilla cook
  5. Add chicken to tortilla and roll it
  6. Add cheese and toppings to liking
